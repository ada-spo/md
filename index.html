How to Delegate

Staking with SUNNE is simple. Follow these steps to start earning rewards.

01

Get a Wallet

Download a Cardano wallet like Daedalus, Yoroi, Typhon, or Eternl. Create a wallet and secure your recovery phrase.

02

Buy & Send ADA

Purchase ADA on an exchange and send it to your wallet address. You need at least 10 ADA to delegate.

03

Find SUNNE

In your wallet's staking section, search for "SUNNE" or paste our pool ID:

pool18jrsaddmrwtrdycydmk3zv0fxawnv0q8xh7f6fyp2r247zz9tc5
04

Delegate & Earn

Confirm the delegation transaction. Your ADA stays in your wallet — you never lose control. Rewards arrive every epoch (~5 days).
